Our classes

Hatha Yoga

This class includes following asanas: static postures combined with breathins and concentration. The focus in on developing flexibility and maintaining healthy joints, specially the spine.

Runtime: 90 minutes (1.5 hours)

Experience Level: Any)

Therapy Yoga

Based on attention concentration. It improves health, smooths out of the emotions, speeds up the metabolic processes and normalize the internal organs work.

Runtime: 120 minutes (2 hours)

Experience Level: Any)

Yin Yoga

There are no fixed sequences in this direction, asanas can be performed in any style. Movements are syncrhonized with breathins, posses are flowing smoothly from one to another without a strict secuence.

Runtime: 90 minutes (1.5 hours)

Experience Level: Any)

Power Yoga

A dynamic style in yoga. The hallmarks are the synchronization of inhalation and exhalation with movements, concentration of gaze on certain points of space, muscular "locks" and strictly defined series of asanas.

Runtime: 90 minutes (1.5 hours)

Experience Level: Any)
